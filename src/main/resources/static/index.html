About Our Conference Management System

"Our conference management system is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline every aspect of conference organization."

Read More

Conference Management

Conference Setup

Organizers can set up and customize conference details such as title, description, venue, dates, and schedules.

Call for Papers

Authors can submit papers or abstracts through an online portal, including title, abstract, keywords, and file uploads.

Review and Selection

Our Conference Management System implements a double-blind review system to ensure unbiased feedback. It facilitates both automatic and manual assignment of papers to reviewers based on their expertise. Reviewers can score submissions and provide feedback or recommendations for acceptance, revision, or rejection.

Program Scheduling

Our Conference Management System allows organizers to create sessions based on themes, topics, or types of presentations (e.g., keynote, workshop, poster).
